The Vision and Eye Movements Lab develops methods and systems to measure and analyze eye movements and visual scanning patterns. The systems are used in studies of the visual and oculomotor systems in humans and for the evaluation of cognitive and neuropsychiatric functions. The group is led by Prof. Moshe Eizenman who has appointments in both the departments of Medicine and Engineering at the University of Toronto.
